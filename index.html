About Me

My full name is Khezy Gwen Pantaleon Mangubat, and I live alone in the city to study at my university. As the second daughter in a family of four siblings, I value the bonds that shaped who I am today.

I believe that "less is more," finding joy in the simplicity of life—like warm summer days and cozy moments. Right now, my comfort comes from enjoying McDonald's barbecue fries or chilling with my best friend in our PJs. I’m a fan of F.R.I.E.N.D.S and romcoms, which remind me of the importance of laughter and genuine connections.

I appreciate the beauty in simplicity and fresh flowers bring a smile to my face. Also, I'm passionate about learning in web design, striving to create spaces that feel inviting and authentic.

Seeing how a simple button on a screen could perform complex actions sparked my curiosity, driving me to learn more about web design and development. It wasn’t just about using technology—it was about understanding the magic behind it and creating experiences that could come to life with a click.
